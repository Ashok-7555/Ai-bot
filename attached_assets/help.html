Getting Started with GAKR AI

How to Use GAKR AI

GAKR AI is an advanced conversational assistant designed to help you with a variety of tasks. Here's how to get started:

  1. Creating an Account: Sign up for a free account to save your chat history and personalize your experience.
  2. Starting a Chat: Navigate to the Chat page and type your question or prompt in the input field.
  3. Choosing a Model: Select between Enhanced and Simple models based on your needs.
  4. Asking Questions: You can ask about AI, programming, and various other topics covered in our training datasets.
  5. Viewing History: Access your previous conversations in the History section.

Tips for Effective Conversations

Get the most out of GAKR AI with these helpful tips:

  • Be specific: The more details you provide, the more targeted the response will be.
  • Use the right model: Enhanced model for complex questions, Simple model for basic queries.
  • Maintain context: GAKR AI remembers previous messages in your conversation to provide contextual responses.
  • Adjust settings: Customize the number of messages kept in context to improve relevance.
  • Try different topics: Filter by topics like AI & ML, Programming, or Datasets for specialized knowledge.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is GAKR AI?
GAKR AI is an advanced conversational assistant that uses local inference capabilities to provide responses without relying on external APIs. It's designed to help with various topics including AI, programming, and data science.
What datasets is GAKR AI trained on?
GAKR AI is trained on 139 specialized examples from several datasets, including AI Technical Data (72 examples), Cornell Movie Dialogs (25 examples), Training Datasets Information (15 examples), and AI Capabilities Reference (27 examples).
What's the difference between Enhanced and Simple models?
The Enhanced model uses advanced pattern recognition with better context handling, making it suitable for complex questions. The Simple model provides faster responses with basic functionality, ideal for straightforward queries.
Is GAKR AI free to use?
Yes, GAKR AI offers a free plan with basic features. Premium plans with additional features and higher usage limits are also available.
Does GAKR AI work offline?
Yes, GAKR AI is designed with local inference capabilities, meaning core functionality works without external API dependencies. However, an internet connection is required to access the web interface.
