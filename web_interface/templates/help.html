GAKR AI Help Center

Welcome to the GAKR AI help center. Here you'll find information on how to use the chatbot and get the most out of your experience.

Getting Started

What is GAKR AI?

GAKR AI is a chatbot powered by advanced transformer models that can understand and respond to a wide variety of prompts and questions. It's designed to provide helpful, informative responses without requiring external API calls.

How do I start using GAKR?

You can start using GAKR right away:

  1. Visit the home page
  2. Click the "Try as Guest" button (or login/register if you prefer)
  3. Type your message in the chat box and press Enter
  4. GAKR will respond to your message

Chat Interface

Using the Chat

The chat interface is straightforward:

  • Your messages appear on the right side (in blue)
  • GAKR's responses appear on the left side
  • Type in the input box at the bottom and press Enter to send
  • Conversations are saved automatically for registered users
Guest Mode Limitations

As a guest, you can have a limited number of exchanges (typically 5) before being prompted to register or log in. Guest conversations are stored only in your browser session.

User Accounts

Why Register?

Registered users enjoy these benefits:

  • Unlimited conversations
  • Conversation history saved across sessions
  • Customizable settings and preferences
  • Ability to pick up conversations where you left off
How to Register

Registration is simple:

  1. Click the "Register" button in the navigation bar
  2. Fill in the required information (username, email, password)
  3. Click "Signup"
  4. You'll be automatically logged in and ready to chat

Conversation History

Viewing Past Conversations

Registered users can access their conversation history:

  1. Click "History" in the navigation bar
  2. Browse through your past conversations
  3. Click on any conversation to continue it
Managing History

You can manage your conversation history:

  • Delete individual conversations using the trash icon
  • Clear all history using the "Clear All" button

Settings

Customizing Your Experience

Registered users can customize their GAKR experience:

  • Theme: Choose between Light, Dark, or Blue themes
  • AI Model: Select which underlying model to use
  • Spell Check: Enable or disable automatic spelling correction
Accessing Settings

To change your settings:

  1. Click on your username in the navigation bar
  2. Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu
  3. Adjust your preferences
  4. Click "Save Settings"

Tips for Better Results

Getting the Best Responses

To get better responses from GAKR:

  • Be specific: Clear, specific questions get better answers
  • Provide context: Include relevant background information
  • Follow up: Ask follow-up questions to refine responses
  • Break down complex queries: Ask one thing at a time for complex topics
Example Queries

Here are some example prompts that work well:

  • "Explain quantum computing in simple terms"
  • "What are three ways to improve my time management?"
  • "Write a short poem about autumn"
  • "Help me troubleshoot why my sourdough bread isn't rising"

Technical Information

How GAKR Works

GAKR AI uses advanced transformer-based language models to generate responses. Key features include:

  • Offline processing (no external API calls)
  • Contextual understanding of conversations
  • Spelling correction for improved input handling
  • Multiple model options with different capabilities
Privacy & Data

GAKR respects your privacy:

  • All processing happens on our servers
  • We don't share your conversations with third parties
  • Your data is used only to provide the service and improve the system
